Welcome

This is my corner of the web, a messy little zine where colors are matte, textures are grainy, and nothing is perfectly aligned.

Think of it as an old sketchbook I glued to a website, with sticky notes, doodles, and bits of randomness sprinkled around.

Daily Musings

- Watching old movies in grainy quality is oddly comforting.

- Sometimes I make little graphics in Paint and never show them to anyone.

- The world looks better when colors are muted, like dusty pastels or old photographs.

- I like collecting strange sounds, snippets of code, and unreadable ASCII art.

Things I Collect

- Old webpages that feel alive.

- Sticky notes from previous decades.

- Matte colored pens and paper scraps.

- Small glitches that make a computer feel like it's thinking.

- Light blue skies at dawn, the softest tones that are not too bright.

~

Notes to Self

• Perfection is boring.

• Embrace the messy, the crooked, the torn edges.

• Add more blue, dusty pastels, and orange highlights to give life to matte tones.

• ASCII and doodles make a page feel human again.
